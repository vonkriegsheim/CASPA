Species: Human
Tissue: Prenatal brain (prefrontal cortex and nascent frontal telencephalon)
Developmental stage: Fetal — gestational weeks 13, 15, and 19
  GW13: entire nascent frontal telencephalon (including ganglionic eminences)
  GW15 and GW19: microdissected germinal zone (GZ) and cortical plate (CP)
    from prefrontal cortex
Instrument: DIA mass spectrometry, DIA-NN search
Expected cell types: Neural progenitor/stem cells (radial glia, intermediate
  progenitors), early neurons (immature excitatory and inhibitory), microglia,
  endothelial cells, pericytes, fibroblasts/meningeal cells
Known biology: At GW13-19 astrocytes have NOT yet differentiated — do NOT use
  the label "astrocyte". Radial glia (SOX2+/FABP7+/VIM+/GFAP+) serve as the
  neural stem cell population and should be annotated as radial glia or neural
  progenitors, not astrocytes. Oligodendrocyte lineage is also largely absent at
  this stage. GABAergic interneurons may be present from ganglionic eminence origin.
