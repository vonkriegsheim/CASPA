Species: Human
Tissue: Skin tumor (CYLD cutaneous syndrome / cylindroma)
Developmental stage: Adult
Condition: Tumor resection, fresh tissue
Sample preparation: Enzymatic digestion (trypsin 40min 37°C, collagenase overnight 4°C),
  FACS sorted into CD45-/CD200+ (tumor keratinocytes) and CD45+/CD74+ (immune/myeloid cells),
  oil-immersion single-cell capture
Instrument: Bruker timsTOF HT, Evosep One LC, Whisper-Zoom 40 SPD
Expected cell types: Tumor keratinocytes (hair follicle origin), macrophages,
  dendritic cells, possibly T/B cells, stromal cells (fibroblasts, pericytes)
Known biology: CCS tumors show CYLD loss leading to NF-kB activation;
  tumor-associated macrophages have been reported in similar hair follicle tumors
